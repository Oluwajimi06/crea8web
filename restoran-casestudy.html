Restoran is a cutting-edge food restaurant web app built on the robust Django framework, designed to redefine the way customers engage with dining establishments. With a focus on seamless user experience and innovative features, Feastify offers a convenient platform for users to discover, order, and enjoy their favorite meals from the comfort of their homes or on-the-go.
